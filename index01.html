Iris Milward

Mobiles are like listening to a poem with your eyes.

In these mobiles I use patterns of words and lines from poems to create a rhythm in space.


I improvise the calligraphy for each poem, in English and Hebrew.

Shaping words by how they sound to me and what they mean.


The poems I use are written by contemporary and ancient poets.

Credits under each poem.
